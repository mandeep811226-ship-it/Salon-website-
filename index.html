A single chair in Dadabari grew into Kota's most-reviewed salon and a working academy — because every client left looking, and feeling, like the best version of themselves.

Manisha Tushar Salon & Academy brings hair colour, bridal makeup, nail artistry and skin care under one roof, led by stylists who treat technique as craft. Alongside the salon floor sits the academy: a classroom for stylists who don't just want a certificate, but a real identity in the beauty industry.

Every appointment is built around one idea — luxury isn't a service, it's a standard, held consistently, chair after chair, bride after bride.

Questions

Before you book.

Walk-ins are welcome, but appointments are recommended for colour, bridal and academy sessions since chair time is limited. Message us on WhatsApp to check same-day availability.

We recommend locking your bridal package and trial sitting several weeks ahead of the wedding date, especially in peak season, so hair, makeup and nail work can be planned together.

Admissions are open on a rolling basis for hairdressing and bridal makeup training. Message the salon directly for current batch dates, course structure and fees.
