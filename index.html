A single chair in Dadabari grew into one of Kota's most-reviewed nail and beauty studios, and a working academy — because every client left looking, and feeling, like the best version of themselves.

Manisha Tushar Salon & Academy brings hair, skin, makeup and nail artistry under one roof, led by artists who treat technique as craft. Alongside the salon floor sits the academy: a working floor where beauticians and hairdressers are trained and hired directly, not taught from a textbook.

Every appointment is built around one idea — luxury isn't a service, it's a standard, held consistently, chair after chair, bride after bride.

Questions

Before you book.

Walk-ins are welcome, but appointments are recommended for colour, bridal and academy sessions since chair time is limited. Message us on WhatsApp to check same-day availability.

We recommend locking your bridal package and trial sitting several weeks ahead of the wedding date, especially in peak season, so hair, makeup and nail work can be planned together.

It's hands-on, paid training on the salon floor — not a classroom course. We're currently hiring Senior Beauticians (₹10,000–₹23,500/month plus incentives, 6-day week) and Unisex Hairdressers (2+ years' experience preferred). Message us on WhatsApp if you'd like to join the team.

Monday–Saturday, 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Sunday hours vary, so it's worth messaging ahead if you're planning a weekend visit.
